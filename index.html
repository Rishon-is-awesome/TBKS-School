1st grade subjects

In first grade our little students will learn addition with standard algorythm and solve problems like 43+57 or 5+30 etc. Students in this grade will (During ELA) will learn how to compare and contrast with venn diagrams. Teachers will also read stories to them and ask questions about the book. During science kids will learn how to do basic coding like drag and drop block commands *NOTE the coding will have full english so that students can understand the code. the last thirty minutes will be focus on sitting in circle on the mat and share how their day is or something else the child wants to say. More information can be found in the school schedule.
